Recipe

Chicken, pesto and rocket sandwich

Ingredients

  • 3-4 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1-2 tbsp pesto
  • 3 cooked skinless chicken breast, shredded
  • handful rocket
  • 6 granary or seeded bread rolls, sliced horizontally, lightly buttered.

Instructions

  • Mix the mayonnaise and pesto together in a bowl. Gently stir in the shredded chicken.
  • Fill the rolls with the chicken mixture and top with some rocket.
