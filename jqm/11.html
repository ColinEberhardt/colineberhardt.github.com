Recipe

Lancashire cheese and red onion marmalade sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 2 red onions, peeled and finely sliced
  • 2-4 tbsp brown sugar
  • butter, for spreading
  • 4-6 slices white bread
  • 150g/5oz Lancashire cheese, sliced, preferably organic

Instructions

  • Heat the oil in a heavy-based frying pan over a medium heat. Add the sliced onions and the sugar. Cook for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the onions are softened and caramelised. Set aside to cool.
  • Butter the bread, remove the crusts and cut into squares.
  • Top half of the bread with the caramelised onions. Arrange the slices of cheese on top and sandwich with the remaining bread.
  • To serve, cut the sandwiches into triangles.
